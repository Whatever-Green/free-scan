This website is an ARCHIVE of all relevant textbooks, directly ripped from multiple sources.

How did you do the Science Textbooks?

All the OCR science books are re-engineered to work with the Kerboodle backend without needing a login. This is possible due to a flaw in how Kerboodle serves image content. Using the iframe element, we can directly pull pages from Kerboodle's servers — without them even noticing. It's faster too, because we stripped all the bloat and tracking Oxford Kerboodle uses.

Why do the other textbooks look terrible?

They’re just plain PDFs, generously "donated" by LIBGEN and ZLIB.PUB online library collections.

How is this hosted?

For free — just HTML, CSS, and JS. It can run almost anywhere.
